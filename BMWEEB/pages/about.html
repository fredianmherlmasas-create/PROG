About BISU-Calape

Bohol Island State University - Calape Campus (BISU-Calape) is one of the external campuses of the Bohol Island State University system. Located in the municipality of Calape, Bohol, our campus has been dedicated to providing quality higher education to students in the northwestern part of Bohol.

The university offers various undergraduate programs in education, business administration, information technology, agriculture, and fisheries. We are committed to producing competent graduates who are equipped with the knowledge and skills needed to contribute to national development.

Our Vision

A premier Science and Technology university for the formation of a world class and virtuous human resource for sustainable development in Bohol and beyond.

Our Mission

BISU is committed to provide quality higher education in the arts and sciences, as well as in the professional and technological fields; undertake research and development and extension services for the sustainable development of Bohol and beyond.

About The Feedback System

Understanding our commitment to service excellence

The Web-Based Customer Feedback Satisfaction System was developed to provide a convenient and efficient platform for gathering feedback from students, faculty, staff, and other stakeholders about the various services offered by BISU-Calape.

This system is part of our continuous quality improvement initiative, aligned with the university's commitment to providing excellent services and achieving higher standards of customer satisfaction.
