Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I submit feedback?

You can submit feedback by clicking on "Submit Feedback" in the navigation menu, selecting the service you want to rate, filling out the form, and clicking submit. The process takes just a few minutes.

Is my feedback anonymous?

You have the option to submit feedback anonymously. Simply check the "Submit as anonymous" option in the feedback form, and your personal information will not be displayed.

How long does it take to get a response?

We review all feedback within 24-48 hours. For urgent matters, you may contact our office directly via phone. Responses to inquiries are typically sent via email within 3-5 business days.

Can I track the status of my feedback?

Currently, feedback status tracking is available through our administrative system. If you need updates on specific feedback, please contact our Quality Assurance office.

What services can I provide feedback on?

You can provide feedback on all campus services including Library, Registrar's Office, Cashier's Office, IT Services, Health Services, Guidance Office, and more.

Who can submit feedback?

Anyone who has used our campus services can submit feedback, including students, faculty, staff, alumni, parents/guardians, and visitors.
